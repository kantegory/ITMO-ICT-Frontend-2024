Pancakes

Recipe Details

Author: John Doe

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Milk
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Make batter: Whisk together flour, sugar, baking powder, milk, eggs, and melted butter until smooth.
  2. Heat pan: Preheat a non-stick skillet over medium heat and lightly grease with butter or oil.
  3. Cook pancakes: Pour batter into the pan, forming circles. Cook until bubbles form, then flip and cook until golden.
  4. Serve: Stack pancakes and top with syrup, fruit, or your favorite toppings.
